# APR-3005 handoff — growth archive and local song import

## Delivered

- Growth records now retain the complete structured report and open through `/growth/:sessionId`.
- The archive exposes latest score, valid sample count, average confidence, and recent-three versus previous-three trend per metric.
- Reports explain weight, measurement method, evidence, confidence, recommendation, interpretation boundary, and evidence timestamps.
- `/songs/import` accepts local audio plus LRC/TXT lyrics, creates a timeline, silent auxiliary tracks, manifest, and IndexedDB song record.
- Imported material is original-song sing-along mode. Pitch practice, rescue, and harmony remain disabled until preprocessing and manual review exist.

## Compatibility

Legacy growth entries retain their summary but cannot reconstruct a full report. The history detail screen states this explicitly.
